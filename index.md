# It's time for a change!

After over twelve years of providing cryptocurrency tracking, reporting, and portfolio
services to the community, we've made the difficult decision to shut down the
CryptFolio web and mobile apps.

Thank you to everyone who has ever supported CryptFolio through this adventure!

\- [Jevon Wright](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jevonwright/), Founder

### If you have been a user of cryptfolio.com:

Please email [accounts@cryptfolio.com](mailto:accounts@cryptfolio.com) ASAP
if you would like an export of any personal data that we may have had associated with your account(s).
Otherwise, all user data that we may have held about you will be permanently deleted on or before 1 January 2027.
